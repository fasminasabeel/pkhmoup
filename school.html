PKHMOUPSCHOOL-EDATHANATTUKARA

PKHMOUP School, officially known as PKHMOUPS Edathanattukara, is an upper primary educational institution located in the village of Edathanattukara, within the Mannarkkad block of Palakkad district, Kerala. Established in 1954 through the efforts of Parokkottil Kunhi Mammu Haji and his sons, the school was later donated to a local orphanage by Hamza Haji. Currently, it operates under the management of this orphanage, providing education to students in grades 5 through 7.
