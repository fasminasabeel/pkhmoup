Our Facilities

Clubs

We offer our students a wide variety of co-curricular activities such as Public Speaking, Debate, Creativity Workshops, Group Discussions, Quizzing, Classes in Drawing, Painting, Philately, Trekking etc. Clubs organise national festivals, quiz, sports day, arts day and other competitions. It is mandatory for each student to be a part of at least one of the clubs. Various clubs functioning in the school are: Science Club Literacy club Reading club Social science club Health club

Computer Lab

Our Computer Lab is equipped with high tech IT facilities like multi-media and internet, enabling students to surf internet and research information. The School has a curriculum and highly qualified teachers for the computer education

Environment

The clean and green environs of the School campus are a treat to the eyes. Being away from the hustle and bustle of the town, the campus remains peaceful and calm which is essential for imparting quality education

Library

The School has a spacious library with books on a variety of subjects and periodicals for issue and reference. The school subscribes to number of periodicals in English and Malayalam. All the collections in the library are classified and catalogued according to the scientific schemes.. Every year new books are added to the library.

Sports and Games

School gives adequate coaching for sports and games and athletics to increase the physical and mutual endurance among the students. The school has won many titles in athletics in district level. It has a stable Football team, Badminton team and a Volleyball team.

Health & Wellness

The School health services are aimed at creating a sage and healthful environment for each student. Our goal is to provide an optimal, healthy environment for students to learn. This is achieved by a collaboration of the following element of school health. First Aid Health Screening Health Education For emergencies a doctor is available nearby.

Physical Education

The school aims to develop control, co-ordination and mastery of the body, learnt through action, sensation and observation. Opportunities are provided to grow and develop through games & athletics under qualified teachers and special coaching in Cricket, Football, Badminton etc

>

Science Lab

The school has a science lab is a workplace for the conduct of scientific research. It helps students to remember the concepts better and to transfer the experience to real-life situations. It is a facility that provides controlled conditions in which scientific research, experiments and measurements may be performed.

Playground

The school has spacious grounds for Football, Badminton ,Cricket & Volley ball etc.

Transport

School bus is running to bring students to school and back their home from far and near
