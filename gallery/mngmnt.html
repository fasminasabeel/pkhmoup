Management's Message

“Children are the future and the most valued treasure of a strong and prosperous nation. According to Swami Vivekananda, education means that process by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased, and intellect is sharpened, as a result of which one can stand on one’s own feet.”

As our motto says, PKHMOUPS always seeks the light from Above to serve the students and their parents. The pupils are being trained to stand on their own feet through the care of the teaching and non-teaching staff.

Being nurtured and nourished in PKHMOUPS, our students throw light on many lives in independent parts of the world.
